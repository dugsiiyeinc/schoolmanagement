Our History

Garaad Secondary School was founded with the mission of delivering high-quality secondary education to prepare students for higher education.

Over the years, the school has grown into a trusted institution known for academic excellence, discipline, and student success.

Today, we continue to provide modern education that empowers students to succeed in university and professional careers.
